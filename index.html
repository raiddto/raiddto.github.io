Compass Booking

Compass Booking is an innovative website that provides users with a seamless experience in booking and reserving ski cabins and equipment online. With easy registration and subscription processes, users can quickly access a wide range of available cabins and equipment and make reservations in just a few clicks. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, making it easy for both novice and experienced skiers to find the right cabin and equipment to suit their needs. Overall, Compass Booking is a one-stop-shop for all your skiing needs, offering a convenient and hassle-free way to plan your next winter adventure.

Hospital Hospice CC

A hospice website provides valuable information and resources for individuals and families dealing with serious illness or end-of-life care. The website typically offers information about hospice services, support programs, and resources for both patients and caregivers. The website may also include tools to help patients and families navigate the hospice care process, such as directories of local hospice providers, FAQs, and online forums for support and advice. In addition to information and resources, many hospice websites offer ways to donate or volunteer to support their mission of providing compassionate care to those in need. Overall, a hospice website serves as a valuable resource for those facing difficult end-of-life decisions and provides a supportive and caring community for individuals and families during this challenging time.

Users can easily browse through different tour packages that cater to different interests, budgets, and group sizes. The website may also offer additional services such as hotel accommodations, meals, and guided tours. Overall, a travel and tour website is a one-stop-shop for all your travel needs, offering a convenient and cost-effective way to plan your next adventure.

PhIIRE

Document Identification and Tracking System (DoITS) for the Provincial Engineering Office (PEO). DoITS is a powerful document tracking system that I designed from the ground up, which includes a database and many advanced features. One of the key additions I made to the system was the integration of Geographic Information System (GIS) technology, which provides a transparent and user-friendly way for users to view details of PEO projects. With GIS, users can easily browse a map of the area and click on specific projects to view all relevant information. This functionality is similar to Google Maps, and it greatly enhances the user experience and overall effectiveness of the DoITS system.
